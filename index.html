And now with House Band for Amazon Alexa, you can start listening to the music on your media center server faster than ever. "Alexa, open House Band..."

Play your music

You can play songs based on key words:

"Alexa, play Adele" will build a playlist with all your music by Adele!

"Alexa, play live" will build a playlist of all albums (songs, etc) with the word "live" in their title!

Or build a playist by album or artist

"Alexa, ask House Band to play the album 19 by Adele."

"Alexa, ask House Band to play the album Blue Train by John Coltrane."

"Alexa, ask House Band to play songs by Vance Joy."

Search your catalog

"Alexa, launch House Band. What do I have by Ray Charles?"

"Alexa, ask House Band what I have by Mahler."
